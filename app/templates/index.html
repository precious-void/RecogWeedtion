Plant Seedlings Recognition

How to forget weeds problem in your gardenside?

We created a special app, that would help you recognizing, weather it`s a cultural plant or a weed.
The working principle of our service: «Just upload photo of a plant and we`d make a decision».

How it works?

We have a special Data Base of plants and weeds, current list of them will be presented below.
According to our DB we analyze your uploaded photo with some special machine learing algorithms and, finaly, make a decision, which defines attachment of a shooted plant to any plant variety from our DB.

Varieties of plants represented in our DB nowadays

In total we have 12 sorts of plants in our DB, which includes 5 cultivar crops and 7 weeds.
